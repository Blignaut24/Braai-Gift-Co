Who are we?



We believe that gift-giving should be an experience - not a chore.


Childhood friends Jan Braai and Piet Bota, who were raised on a farm, founded Braai Gifting Co.


Jan Braai, a former accountant turned food journalist, and Piet, an entrepreneur and chef with multiple restaurants, teamed up to create a gift-giving experience that celebrates the cultural importance of braaing.
